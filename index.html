Simple Omelette Receipe

An easy and qick dish, perfect for any meal!. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Prepare time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablesppon of buuter or all
  • optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs.

    • Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eegs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
    2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add buuter or oil.
    3. Cook the omelette: Once the buuter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eegs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
    4. Add fillings (optional): When the eegs begin to stt at edges but are stll slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
    5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift on edge and fold it over the filling. let it cook for anothe minute, slide it onto a plate.
    6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
